Frequently Asked Questions

What services does IQvention offer?

IQvention offers a wide range of services including web design, web development, e-commerce solutions, SEO optimization, content marketing, social media management, analytics & reporting, and mobile app development.

How long does it take to complete a project?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. A basic website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 2-3 months or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your website or application continues to perform optimally. The duration of support is included in each of our packages, with options to extend.

Can you help improve our existing website?

We offer website audits and optimization services to improve the performance, design, and functionality of existing websites. We'll analyze your current site and provide recommendations for improvement.

What is your approach to SEO?

Our SEO approach is comprehensive and tailored to each client. We focus on on-page optimization, quality content creation, technical SEO, link building, and continuous monitoring and adjustment to improve your search engine rankings.

Do you offer custom solutions?

Yes, we specialize in creating custom solutions tailored to your specific business needs. Whether it's a unique web application, a custom e-commerce platform, or a bespoke content management system, we can build it for you.
